Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our hotel and services.

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is from 2:00 PM onwards, and check-out is until 12:00 PM. Early check-in and late check-out are available upon request, subject to availability.

Do you offer airport transfers?

Yes, we offer complimentary airport transfers for guests staying in Executive and Presidential Suites. For other rooms, airport transfer can be arranged at an additional charge.

Is there free WiFi available?

Yes, complimentary high-speed WiFi is available throughout the hotel for all guests.

Do you have parking facilities?

Yes, we have a secure underground parking facility with valet service available 24/7 for our guests.

Are pets allowed?

We understand that pets are family too. We welcome small pets with prior arrangement. Additional charges may apply.

What dining options are available?

We offer three signature restaurants: Aqua Terrace (Seafood & Grill), Lemon Garden (Organic & Fresh), and Black & White (Fine Dining).
